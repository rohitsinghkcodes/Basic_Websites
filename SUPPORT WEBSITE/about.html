What's This Project Is All About?

This is an Website for local citizens to volunteer or provide the best help that needy people can get from the local NGOs .This app allows local people to directly interact with the NGOs and provide such things which are no longer useful for them.Like old furniture ,books ,toys for children etc.By this app we can also donate the extra food that remains at our home after any kind of function .In the same way restaurants can also contact to the NGOs directly For donation of their extra food.People can also donate books to the poor children which will contribute a major role in enhancing their knowledge.The main motive of this app is to help all the needy or poor people in the best manner through NGOs
